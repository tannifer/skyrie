About Grayscale

Grayscale is a free Bootstrap 3 theme created by Start Bootstrap. It can be yours right now, simply download the template on the preview page. The theme is open source, and you can use it for any purpose, personal or commercial.

This theme features stock photos by Gratisography along with a custom Google Maps skin courtesy of Snazzy Maps.

Grayscale includes full HTML, CSS, and custom JavaScript files along with LESS files for easy customization.

Modern Funky Ceilidh

Our line-up is: 2 Fiddles, Guitar and Drums. By request we also play highland pipes, flute, whistle, cittern and mandolin. Our ceilidh set consists of traditional and contemporary Scottish and Irish tunes, and some we’ve written ourselves. If in addition you have any special requests, we are always happy to oblige!

We are all confident callers, so whatever the line-up, we’ll be able to guide you through the dances. We call all the dances and demonstrate them; it is all part of the fun! We play all your favourite ceilidh dances, and add a few surprises.

  • Gay Gordons
  • Canadian Barn Dance (ordinary and progressive)
  • Heel Toe Polka
  • Dashing White Sergeant
  • Virginia Reel
  • Circassian Circle
  • Eightsome Reel
  • Strip the Willow
  • Orcadian Strip the Willow
  • Flying Scotsman
  • Military 2-step
  • St Bernard’s Waltz
  • Pride of Erin Waltz
  • Scottish Waltz
  • Highland Scottish
