The Band

Lesley Thompson

Lesley has performed several times on STV and has worked as a session musician performing across Europe in a variety of genres: Folk, Bluegrass, Trad and Gypsy. She also teaches, composes Scottish fiddle tunes, and has competed at the highest level in Scots fiddle, featuring as a Glenfiddich finalist for 3 years 2011-2014 and being invited to championships across Scotland. Lesley plays piano, bagpipes, whistle, mandolin and guitar.

Hannah Coutts

Hannah grew up learning fiddle in the pubs of Liverpool before relocating to Scotland at 18. Her playing has absorbed the best of Scottish, Irish and English influences to create a rhythmic sound filled with exciting harmonies and infectious enthusiasm. Hannah has played with numerous bands in a range of genres, from pure traditional folk to folk rock and heavy metal.

Chris Smith

Chris spent 15 years as a session drummer recording, performing and touring with many artists including: Big Country, Nazareth, Dougie Maclean, Kathy Mattea, The Platters, Maggie Riley and Martin Taylor.

Chris also worked as a composer of neo-classical music and his work has featured on tv channels: Classic FM TV, o Music, C Music, the Performance Channel, Al Jazeera and CNN. Chris collaborated with Emmy Award winning Sex and the City producer Michael King.

Ceilidh

Skyrie are a premium Scottish ceilidh band playing traditional music with a modern twist. We are based in Fife and Angus but happy to travel all over Scotland and beyond.

We regularly play in Forgan's, St Andrews, Broughty Ferry, Dundee and Perth.

Our standard line-up is two fiddles, guitar and drums. By request we also play bagpipes, flute, whistle and mandolin. Our ceilidh set consists of traditional and contemporary Scottish and Irish tunes, and some we’ve written ourselves. If in addition you have any special requests, we are always happy to oblige!

We call all the dances and demonstrate them, so first-timers are always welcome! We play all your favourite ceilidh dances, and add a few surprises to keep things exciting.

  • Gay Gordons
  • Canadian Barn Dance (standard and progressive)
  • Heel Toe Polka
  • Dashing White Sergeant
  • Virginia Reel
  • Circassian Circle
  • Eightsome Reel
  • Strip the Willow (standard and orcadian)
  • Flying Scotsman
  • Military Twostep
  • St Bernard’s Waltz
  • Pride of Erin Waltz
  • Scottish Waltz
  • Highland Schottische
